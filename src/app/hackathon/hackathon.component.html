Our Goal

Inspire youth, parents, and mentors to join together and use technology to change South Central LA for the better. We believe computer science is a tool for social change. Your app could help improve your church, school, block, park, or any part of your community. If you are between the ages of 9 and 17, come and build a cool website or app with us!

Volunteer

If you are a professional developer, UX designer, or someone with a passion for mentoring Black and Brown youth in tech, we need your help. Our goal is to have 100 developers who will spark the mind of those who will change the world! Join us as a mentor and bring a friend to help us inspire kids who have never coded in their life. Sign up today and be part of the movement!
