Providing Opportunities for South Central Youth

TXT Labs is a consulting firm specializing in software development & IT services, focused on providing end-to-end solutions, including ideation, development, and marketing. Founded in 2016, it provides clients a wide range of industry-leading products and services, utilizing our extensive expertise in the tech and educational sectors.

TXT Labs improves the lives of traditionally underrepresented youth in L.A. by providing them with instruction and experience in design, software development, and entrepreneurship, while creating job opportunities. By working with us, you are providing 21st century youth and workforce development opportunities and experience to at-risk youth in our most challenging communities. All profits go back into the community and fund Teens Exploring Technology’s Coding Leadership Academies.

The organization provides outstanding service to business for web and mobile app development, IT consulting services, and Educational STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) consulting. The team is committed to improving your business’s efficiency and profitability through innovative technology.
