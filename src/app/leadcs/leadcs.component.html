Learn how to build dynamic apps

Run by TXT and volunteers from the USC Iovine and Young Academy, Leadership Computer Science (Lead CS) introduces middle school boys of color to computer science, entrepreneurship, and tech careers. Come learn how to build your own app on a college campus with state-of-the-art tech equipment. Our students make real-world, hands-on tech projects for their friends, families, and communities. You too can become a tech entrepreneur and earn prizes for your great idea.

Meet professionals in the field

You'll also get to meet inspiring tech leaders and visit cool companies like Google and Disney. We meet at the USC campus for eight weeks on Saturdays from 10-3 pm. Free lunch and snacks are provided. This program is held every quarter.
